# Business Idea Brief

## Niche statement

AI-led buyer pipeline systems for family-led Indian manufacturing SMEs selling high-ticket machinery or industrial products, whose leads are stuck across offline channels, IndiaMART, TradeIndia, WhatsApp, weak websites, and founder relationships, so they can filter junk enquiries, follow up on serious buyers, and build a trackable RFQ pipeline for India and export markets.

## Validation score: 77/100

- Pain intensity: 8/10 - Sellers publicly complain about fake or low-quality marketplace leads, poor reporting, and slow follow-up; this matches the founder's own past IndiaMART, Justdial, and TradeIndia experience.
- Purchasing power: 7/10 - Manufacturing-specific packages are publicly priced from INR 9,999 to INR 39,999 per month, Indian B2B GTM retainers appear around INR 1.5L to INR 3L per month, and broader B2B lead-generation agencies commonly charge USD 3,000 to USD 12,000 per month.
- Ease to find: 6/10 - Manufacturing SMEs are findable through IndiaMART, TradeIndia, industrial clusters, LinkedIn, association directories, and referrals, but the founder's long-run network in this specific niche is still thin.
- Market growth: 8/10 - IndiaMART reports millions of seller storefronts and annual enquiries, GeM is showing major MSME procurement participation, and NITI Aayog has highlighted MSME exports and e-commerce export enablement as a policy opportunity.
- Competition difficulty: 6/10 - Generic lead-gen, digital marketing, marketplace, and CRM options already exist, but the orchestration layer across lead sources, qualification, follow-up, product storytelling, and founder-readable ROI is less clearly owned.
- Founder-market fit: 7/10 - The founder has direct experience with junk marketplace leads, B2B system integration, AI growth console architecture, AI creative production, and technical product visualization; the main gap is deeper manufacturing proof.

Composite score = 46 x 1.67 = 76.8, rounded to 77/100.

Verdict: Solid. Proceed with a pilot-first offer, but treat distribution and manufacturing proof as the main risks to solve in the first 30 days.

## Market findings

- Saturated: Generic "AI-first digital agency," social media posting, simple website development, IndiaMART optimization alone, and cheap lead-generation packages.
- Gap to own: A central buyer pipeline architecture that captures, qualifies, follows up, and reports on enquiries across website/RFQ forms, IndiaMART, TradeIndia, WhatsApp, GeM readiness, referrals, and offline interactions.
- Why now: B2B marketplaces are adopting AI and vernacular discovery; GeM is becoming a serious MSME procurement channel; manufacturers increasingly need digital buyer reach, especially for export and non-local markets.

## Unfair advantage

You have personally experienced the exact failure mode this market suffers from: an abundance of marketplace leads where 90 percent or more feel fake, which causes founders and sales teams to lose trust in the entire channel and miss the few valuable buyers hidden inside the noise.

Your advantage is not deep manufacturing heritage yet. It is the ability to centralize a messy B2B growth architecture, connect tools instead of replacing them, and create a system where serious enquiries are captured, qualified, followed up, and made visible to the founder. The added edge is technical product storytelling: you have worked on AI films, solution videos, and digital-twin-style product explanation, which matters for machinery buyers who need to understand how a product works before committing.

## Offer

Recommended tier: Engine - INR 1.75L/month, minimum 3 months.

Deliverables:

- Lead source audit across website, IndiaMART, TradeIndia, WhatsApp, offline/exhibition leads, referrals, and spreadsheets.
- Buyer pipeline dashboard with source, quality score, urgency, owner, follow-up status, and next action.
- AI-assisted lead qualification and follow-up workflows for WhatsApp, email, and internal sales review.
- Website/RFQ landing page improvements for 1-2 priority products.
- Product storytelling support: product page narrative, explainer structure, and buyer-facing sales collateral.
- Weekly pipeline review and founder-readable ROI report.
- Team handover and operating playbook.

Minimum commitment: 3 months.

## One-liner

"I help family-led Indian manufacturing SMEs turn scattered IndiaMART, TradeIndia, WhatsApp, website, and offline enquiries into a trackable buyer pipeline using AI-assisted lead qualification, follow-up automation, and technical product storytelling."

## 7-day action plan

- Days 1-2: Create a 12-question Manufacturing Buyer Pipeline Audit covering lead sources, response time, lead quality, follow-up ownership, website/RFQ readiness, product explanation, marketplace usage, and reporting.
- Day 3: Build a one-page diagnostic scorecard with 5 dimensions: lead capture, qualification, follow-up, product clarity, ROI visibility.
- Days 4-5: Speak with 5 manufacturing SME founders or sales heads from existing references. Do not pitch first; diagnose their lead flow and ask for screenshots/workflow examples where possible.
- Day 6: Convert the strongest conversation into a paid 14-day Foundation Sprint proposal at INR 75K-INR 1.25L depending on scope.
- Day 7: Publish one LinkedIn post or founder note: "The problem is not more industrial leads. The problem is knowing which 3 out of 100 are worth chasing."

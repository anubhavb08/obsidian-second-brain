# Zlicc HQ

This is the main dashboard for Zlicc intelligence.

## Core Thesis

Zlicc should be understood as an experience infrastructure company, not a scattered set of event-tech services.

The core source says Zlicc builds experience infrastructure for live, hybrid, immersive, intelligent, and screen-led brand moments. The practical marketing task is to translate that into audience-specific offers, campaign assets, sales narratives, and proof.

Primary source: [[10 Raw Sources/Zlicc Marketing/Zlicc_CMO_Brain|Zlicc CMO Brain]]

## Pillar Map

- ZliccSync: registration, access, operational flow, lead capture, dashboards, CRM/API exports.
- ZliccPulse: live audience engagement, polls, quizzes, Q&A, leaderboards, screen-led participation.
- ZliccAI: digital humans, personalization, captions, translation, summaries, knowledge assistants, avatar studios.
- ZliccXR: AR, VR, MR, immersive booths, virtual showrooms, 3D product viewers, projection mapping.
- ZliccLive: virtual, hybrid, webcast, AGM, secure streaming, remote speaker, and archive layer.
- ZliccStudio: anamorphic content, projection mapping media, launch films, kinetic wall content, AI storytelling, special-format screens.

Related wiki page: [[Zlicc Brand And Website Intelligence]]

## Growth System

[[Zlicc Growth Console]] is the main productized growth operating layer for Zlicc. It is designed as a pre-CRM system for campaign planning, prospecting, content, outreach, warm signals, approvals, and qualified lead handoff.

Use [[Zlicc Operating Layer]] as the day-to-day operating dashboard. It connects campaign focus, audience mapping, proof, weekly review, Growth Console decisions, and pillar pages.

## Current Guardrails

- Do not publish public content without approval.
- Do not promise pricing, delivery timelines, technical guarantees, or client references without human review.
- Keep Zlicc and [[Growth Automation Studio]] separate unless deliberately comparing them.

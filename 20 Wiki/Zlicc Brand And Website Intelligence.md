# Zlicc Brand And Website Intelligence

This page collects Zlicc positioning, brand, website, SEO, and content direction.

## Brand Positioning

Zlicc should be positioned as an experience infrastructure company. The market should see one coherent operating layer behind live, hybrid, immersive, AI-led, and screen-led experiences.

Primary source: [[10 Raw Sources/Zlicc Marketing/Zlicc_CMO_Brain|Zlicc CMO Brain]]

## Pillar Navigation

The six-pillar map is the clearest first navigation system:

- ZliccSync
- ZliccPulse
- ZliccAI
- ZliccXR
- ZliccLive
- ZliccStudio

These should be explained through business moments and buyer needs, not only as technology features.

## Working Rules

- Lead with business outcomes and moments.
- Avoid making the pillars feel like unrelated services.
- Keep Zlicc premium, strategic, and execution-capable.
- Avoid generic AI, generic AV, and generic event-agency language.
- Use client logos, names, case studies, and claims only with approval.

## Open Loops

- Turn the pillar map into a polished website navigation.
- Build page-level summaries for ZliccAI, ZliccPulse, ZliccXR, ZliccLive, ZliccSync, and ZliccStudio.
- Create a reusable messaging matrix by audience.
- Create a proof and claims library.

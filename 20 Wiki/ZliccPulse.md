# ZliccPulse

ZliccPulse is Zlicc's live audience engagement layer.

## Simple Line

Makes the room respond.

## Owns

- Single-QR participation.
- Gesture-led interactions.
- Decibel and crowd-noise games.
- Polls.
- Quizzes.
- Q&A.
- Word clouds.
- Selfie walls.
- Leaderboards.
- Reactions.
- Crowd reveals.
- Big-screen engagement.
- Activity analytics.

## Should Not Own

- Registration.
- Access control.
- CRM.
- AI summaries.
- Digital humans.

## Best-Fit Offers

- Agency Tech Partner Stack.
- Audience Engagement Suite.
- Immersive Booth Stack.
- Product Launch Experience Stack.

## Message

A room should not be measured only by attendance. It should be measured by response: QR actions, gestures, cheers, sound levels, team energy, and big-screen participation.

# ZliccXR

ZliccXR is Zlicc's immersive and spatial experience layer.

## Simple Line

Step into the brand.

## Owns

- AR.
- VR.
- MR.
- 3D product viewers.
- Virtual showrooms.
- Hologram-style displays.
- Immersive booths.
- Projection mapping.
- Digital twins.
- Spatial installations.

## Should Not Own

- Registration.
- Payments.
- Polls.
- AI summaries.
- Generic content calendars.

## Best-Fit Offers

- Agency Tech Partner Stack.
- Immersive Activation Stack.
- Immersive Booth Stack.
- Product Launch Experience Stack.
- Immersive Sales Experience Stack.

## Message

The strongest booths are no longer stalls. They are guided product worlds.

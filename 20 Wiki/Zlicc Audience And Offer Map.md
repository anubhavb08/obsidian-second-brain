# Zlicc Audience And Offer Map

This page maps Zlicc audiences to the most useful entry offer and pillar stack.

Primary source: [[10 Raw Sources/Zlicc Marketing/Zlicc_CMO_Brain#6 Primary Audiences|Primary Audiences]]

## Priority Audiences

| Audience | Pain | Entry Offer | Pillars | Message Angle |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Event agencies | Need stronger tech layers in proposals | Agency Tech Partner Stack | Pulse, XR, Studio, Sync | Add high-impact technology layers without building tech internally. |
| Experiential agencies | Need differentiated activations | Immersive Activation Stack | XR, Studio, Pulse, AI | Turn activations into interactive, measurable experience zones. |
| Corporate marketing teams | Need premium measurable brand events | Enterprise Experience Stack | Sync, Pulse, Studio, AI | Make every brand moment spectacular, measurable, and easier to run. |
| Internal communications teams | Need controlled engaging town halls and AGMs | Corporate Communication Stack | Live, Sync, Pulse, AI | Turn communication into secure, interactive, broadcast-ready experience. |
| Exhibition and booth agencies | Need visitor capture and reporting | Booth Intelligence Stack | Sync, XR, Pulse, AI | Turn booth footfall into journeys, leads, insights, and demos. |
| Product launch teams | Need reveal moments and product education | Launch Experience Stack | Studio, XR, Live, Pulse | Launch with cinematic storytelling, immersive demos, and live energy. |
| BFSI and regulated enterprises | Need secure access, control, trust, reporting | Secure Enterprise Event Stack | Sync, Live, AI | Deliver secure, polished, measurable events for sensitive audiences. |

When a new lead, brief, or campaign idea appears:

1. Identify the audience.
2. Pick the closest entry offer.
3. Link the right pillar pages.
4. Check [[Zlicc Proof Library]] for usable evidence.
5. Send the opportunity into [[Zlicc Campaign Focus]] or [[Zlicc Growth Console]].

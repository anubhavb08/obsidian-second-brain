## Proof Tags To Track

- Client or brand name approval status.
- Logo usage approval status.
- Photo/video usage permission.
- Event type.
- Pillars involved.
- Business result or operational proof.
- Sensitive claims requiring review.
- Last reviewed date.

## Proof Rules

- Do not use client names, logos, or case studies unless approved.
- Do not invent implemented capabilities.
- Separate raw proof assets from public-ready claims.
- Each public proof point should have a source, approval status, and owner.

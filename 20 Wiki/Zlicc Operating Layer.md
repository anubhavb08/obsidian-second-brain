# Zlicc Operating Layer

This is the working operating page for running Zlicc as a clearer marketing, sales, and intelligence system.

## Operating Thesis

Zlicc should behave like one experience infrastructure company with multiple deployable layers, not a scattered menu of technology services.

The weekly system should answer:

1. Which audience should Zlicc speak to now?
2. Which problem should lead the message?
3. Which Zlicc pillar or stack solves that problem best?
4. Which campaign asset will move the conversation forward?
5. What should sales, leadership, and delivery do next?

## First Operating Priority

The first campaign recommendation in the CMO source is the **Agency Tech Partner Stack**.

Recommended initial focus:

- Audience: event agencies and experiential agencies.
- Offer: Agency Tech Partner Stack.
- Pillars: [[ZliccPulse]], [[ZliccXR]], [[ZliccStudio]], [[ZliccSync]].
- Core idea: agencies can add technology, interaction, AI, XR, and reporting to proposals without building the technology internally.

Related source section: [[10 Raw Sources/Zlicc Marketing/Zlicc_CMO_Brain#23 First Campaign Recommendation|First Campaign Recommendation]]

## Weekly Rhythm

- Monday: decide the weekly audience, offer, and campaign focus.
- Tuesday: create campaign copy, angles, and asset drafts.
- Wednesday: package collateral, decks, PDFs, landing pages, and proof.
- Thursday: run outreach, follow-ups, LinkedIn work, WhatsApp handoffs, and warm-signal review.
- Friday: review metrics, blockers, replies, content performance, and next week's decision.

Related page: [[Zlicc Weekly Review]]

## Decision Rules

- If the buyer is an event agency, lead with agency tech partner language.
- If the buyer needs room energy, lead with [[ZliccPulse]].
- If the buyer needs AI-led experience, lead with [[ZliccAI]].
- If the buyer is exhibition or booth-led, lead with booth intelligence across [[ZliccSync]], [[ZliccXR]], [[ZliccPulse]], and [[ZliccAI]].
- If the buyer is BFSI or enterprise communications, lead with secure broadcast, access, reporting, and [[ZliccLive]].
- If the buyer needs cinematic launch impact, lead with [[ZliccStudio]] plus XR, Pulse, Live, and Sync as needed.

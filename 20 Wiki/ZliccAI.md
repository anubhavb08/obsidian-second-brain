# ZliccAI

ZliccAI is Zlicc's experiential intelligence layer.

## Simple Line

Brings intelligence to every moment.

## Owns

- Digital humans.
- AI personalization.
- ZliccMoments.
- Captions.
- Translation.
- Transcription.
- Summaries.
- Knowledge assistants.
- Avatar studios.
- Sentiment and theme analysis.

## Should Not Own

- Generic chatbots.
- Event registration.
- QR games.
- Basic video editing.

## Best-Fit Offers

- AI Event Experience Kit.
- Enterprise Experience Stack.
- Immersive Booth Stack.
- Hybrid AGM and Enterprise Broadcast Suite.

## Message

AI becomes valuable when it improves the live experience, not when it sits in a chatbot window.

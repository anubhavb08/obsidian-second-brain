## Skill Set

### B2B CMO

The B2B CMO skill is the broad enterprise marketing leadership brain.

It covers:

- Enterprise B2B marketing strategy.
- Brand, positioning, category design, and communications.
- Premium design and creative approval across static image, landing page, brochure, collateral, and video.
- Demand generation, lead generation, ABM, events, partner marketing, lifecycle, and customer marketing.
- Sales alignment, RevOps, martech, AI, measurement, budgets, org design, governance, compliance, and board-ready CMO reviews.

Use it when the question needs a CMO-level operating view rather than only one channel.

### Enterprise CTO

The Enterprise CTO skill is the broad enterprise technology leadership brain.

It covers:

- Current and emerging technology stacks across frontend, backend, mobile, data, AI, DevOps, cloud-native infrastructure, enterprise apps, and open-source tooling.
- Leading AI company and model-provider intelligence across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Microsoft, AWS, Meta, Mistral, Cohere, xAI, DeepSeek, Qwen, NVIDIA, Hugging Face, Databricks, Snowflake, Perplexity, Groq, Salesforce, ServiceNow, Adobe, and other important AI ecosystems.
- AI tool selection, model gateways, multi-model architecture, RAG/search, agents, evals, procurement, governance, and monthly AI intelligence operating routines.
- Technology strategy, enterprise architecture, platform strategy, cloud, infrastructure, SRE, DevSecOps, developer experience, and engineering operating systems.
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure choices across AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, OCI, PaaS, BaaS, serverless, Kubernetes, edge, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments.
- Developer, platform, SRE, system administrator, cloud ops, corporate IT, enterprise applications, security, data, and AI team structures under the CTO.
- Product engineering, delivery excellence, quality, release governance, architecture review, modernization, transformation, and technical debt management.
- Cybersecurity, zero trust, secure-by-design engineering, software supply-chain security, privacy, compliance, incident response, resilience, and customer trust evidence.
- Data strategy, analytics, product instrumentation, AI governance, responsible AI, generative AI evaluation, model risk, and AI operating models.
- Enterprise applications, integrations, APIs, automation, low-code governance, open-source tool evaluation, vendor strategy, procurement, technology finance, FinOps, technical due diligence, M&A technology review, and board-ready CTO communication.

Use it when the question needs CTO-level technology leadership rather than marketing, channel, or creative execution.

### LinkedIn Expert

The LinkedIn Expert skill is the specialist operating brain for LinkedIn.

It covers:

- LinkedIn profile, company Page, and individual operating routines.
- Organic content, thought leadership, founder/executive content, and employee advocacy.
- Connection strategy, comment-led relationship building, InMail, manual outreach, and social selling.
- LinkedIn Ads, Campaign Manager, targeting, Matched Audiences, Lead Gen Forms, retargeting, conversion tracking, and lead quality loops.
- Sales Navigator prospecting, account lists, lead lists, saved searches, alerts, Buyer Intent, TeamLink, Account Hub, buying committee mapping, CRM handoff, and aggressive-but-platform-safe Sales Navigator sprints.
- YouTube/transcript-informed LinkedIn lead-generation tactics, with official LinkedIn rules treated as the authority.

Use it when the question needs deep LinkedIn execution rather than broad marketing leadership.

### Creative Director

The Creative Director skill is the premium visual imagination and craft brain.

It covers:

- Creative direction operating systems, visual thesis development, moodboards, concept territories, and art direction.
- Premium image, photography, AI-image, illustration, key visual, and campaign image-set direction.
- Video concepts, treatments, scripts, storyboards, shot lists, motion language, editing rhythm, sound, captions, and cutdowns.
- Decks, brochures, PDFs, reports, one-pagers, proposals, sales collateral, and executive documents.
- Premium design critique, creative approval, accessibility, AI-artifact review, production QA, and rights/brand guardrails.

Use it when the question needs high-end visual concepting, image/video/document craft, or a creative director's approval-quality critique.

## Relationship Between The Skills

- B2B CMO and Enterprise CTO are parallel executive lenses inside Personal Growth.
- B2B CMO is the parent strategic lens for enterprise marketing and growth.
- Enterprise CTO is the parent strategic lens for enterprise technology, architecture, engineering, data, AI, cloud, security, and technical risk.
- LinkedIn Expert is a specialist channel/operator lens.
- Creative Director is a specialist visual craft and concepting lens.
- They are separate skills inside the Personal Growth plugin, not sub-skills embedded inside B2B CMO.
- For enterprise LinkedIn strategy, use B2B CMO for commercial strategy and LinkedIn Expert for channel execution.
- For premium campaign, image, video, deck, brochure, or collateral direction, use B2B CMO for business context and Creative Director for art direction and craft.
- For product, platform, AI, data, architecture, implementation, security, or technical credibility questions, use Enterprise CTO as the technology peer to the CMO.
- For launches, trust proof, technical claims, analytics instrumentation, AI claims, or product-led growth, use B2B CMO and Enterprise CTO together: CMO owns market and growth strategy; CTO owns feasibility, architecture, data, security, and operating risk.

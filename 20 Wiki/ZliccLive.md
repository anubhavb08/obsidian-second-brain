# ZliccLive

ZliccLive is Zlicc's virtual, hybrid, webcast, and broadcast layer.

## Simple Line

Takes the experience beyond the room.

## Owns

- Virtual events.
- Hybrid events.
- Webcasts.
- Virtual AGMs.
- Secure streaming.
- Remote speakers.
- Moderated two-way video.
- Archive.
- Watch analytics.

## Should Not Own

- Ordinary meeting links.
- Uncontrolled video calls.
- Registration ownership.
- XR content ownership.

## Best-Fit Offers

- Hybrid AGM and Enterprise Broadcast Suite.
- Corporate Communication Stack.
- Secure Enterprise Event Stack.
- Product Launch Experience Stack.
- AI Event Experience Kit where captions, translation, transcript, or archive intelligence matter.

## Message

A hybrid event should not feel like a camera pointed at a stage.

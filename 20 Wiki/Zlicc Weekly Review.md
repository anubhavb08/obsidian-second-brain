# Zlicc Weekly Review

This page is the weekly review ritual for [[Zlicc Operating Layer]].

## Monday: Decide

- Pick the audience.
- Pick the offer.
- Pick the campaign focus.
- Confirm active campaign lanes.
- Decide the proof or collateral needed this week.

## Tuesday: Create

- Draft content angles.
- Draft LinkedIn ideas.
- Draft outreach notes.
- Draft or update campaign assets.

## Wednesday: Package

- Turn drafts into decks, PDFs, pitch cards, one-pagers, landing pages, or proof snippets.
- Check approval status.
- Update [[Zlicc Proof Library]].

## Thursday: Outreach

- Run LinkedIn human-in-loop work.
- Send approved email or WhatsApp paths where compliant.
- Review warm signals.
- Create follow-up tasks.

## Friday: Review

- Review replies, meetings, signals, content performance, blockers, and next week's decision.
- Update [[Zlicc Campaign Focus]].
- Update [[Open Loops]].

## Weekly Metrics

- Campaign lanes active.
- Qualified prospects created.
- Agency conversations opened.
- Enterprise conversations opened.
- Reply rate.
- Meetings booked.
- Warm signals acted on within 24 hours.
- Content approval cycle time.
- Collateral created or improved.
- Campaign blockers.

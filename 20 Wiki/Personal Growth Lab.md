## Growth Automation Studio Summary

The seeded product idea is a managed B2B pre-CRM growth system. It moves clients from scattered outreach and ad hoc content into a structured operating layer:

Client Brain -> Campaign Lanes -> AI Campaign Assets -> Outreach Workflows -> Warm Signals -> Qualified Conversation Handoff.

The product is not generic marketing automation. It combines strategy, AI creative production, approved outreach workflows, signal capture, CRM handoff, and a recurring AI CMO review rhythm.

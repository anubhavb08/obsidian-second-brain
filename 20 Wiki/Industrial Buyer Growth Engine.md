# Industrial Buyer Growth Engine

Industrial Buyer Growth Engine is a seed business idea from the Ikigai Pro discussion. It sits inside [[Personal Growth Lab]] as an independent business exploration, separate from Zlicc and separate from the existing B2B partnership business.

## Core thesis

Family-led manufacturing SMEs do not only need more leads. They need a reliable way to discover the right buyers, activate them before and around key sales moments, convert product interest into structured RFQs, and make every serious enquiry visible to the founder and sales team.

## Target customer

Family-led Indian manufacturing SMEs that sell high-ticket machinery or industrial products.

Likely buyer:

- Founder / owner.
- Sales head.
- Export head.
- Second-generation family business operator.
- Director responsible for growth or digital transformation.

## Problem

The business may already have activity across marketplaces, WhatsApp, referrals, website enquiries, exhibitions, cold enquiries, and offline relationships, but the pipeline is not trusted.

Common symptoms:

- Most marketplace enquiries feel low quality or fake.
- Serious buyers are hidden inside noisy channels.
- Website and product pages do not create strong RFQs.
- Product specifications and use cases are not buyer-readable.
- Sales follow-up depends on memory, spreadsheets, or founder intervention.
- Exhibition participation is not supported by enough pre-show buyer activation.
- There is no clear source-level ROI view.

## Offer shape

The offer is a connected buyer growth engine with five modules:

1. Buyer discovery.
2. Targeted campaigns.
3. Digital presence and RFQ conversion.
4. Exhibition build-up.
5. Pipeline console.

The key message:

> We are not here to replace your current tools or team. We centralize the architecture so every serious buyer opportunity is discovered, captured, qualified, owned, followed up, and measured.

## Exhibition boundary

This idea must not become an event or exhibition services business.

Allowed:

- Target-buyer list building before a manufacturing client attends an exhibition.
- Pre-show campaigns to invite probable buyers to the client's booth.
- Meeting-slot support and buyer follow-up workflows.
- Post-show RFQ and quote follow-up discipline.

Not allowed:

- Event production.
- Exhibition booth design or execution.
- Event-tech services.
- Event/exhibition vendor ecosystem services.

## First commercial motion

Start with a 14-day Pipeline Audit Sprint:

- Audit current lead sources and 30-60 days of enquiry samples.
- Map target buyer segments and data sources.
- Review website/RFQ/product page readiness.
- Define lead scoring and sales ownership rules.
- Build a first pipeline view.
- Recommend whether a 90-day Buyer Growth Engine is worth building.

## Validation status

Ikigai Pro validation score: 77/100.

Read: strong enough to test, not strong enough to overclaim. The market pain and buying logic are credible, but distribution and manufacturing-specific proof are still early.

## Proof to build next

- One paid diagnostic sprint.
- One before/after lead-source map.
- One pipeline dashboard screenshot or anonymized mock.
- One story showing how a serious RFQ was captured, qualified, and followed up faster.
- One exhibition build-up example if a manufacturing prospect has an upcoming show.

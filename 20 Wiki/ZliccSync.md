# ZliccSync

ZliccSync is Zlicc's operational infrastructure layer.

## Simple Line

Connects the experience.

## Owns

- Microsites.
- Registration.
- WhatsApp/email automation.
- QR access.
- Check-in.
- Badge printing.
- RFID.
- Face-pass entry.
- Lead capture.
- Dashboards.
- CRM/API exports.

## Should Not Own

- Polls.
- AI avatars.
- XR worlds.
- Broadcast graphics.
- Creative films.

## Best-Fit Offers

- Agency Tech Partner Stack.
- Enterprise Experience Stack.
- Booth Intelligence Stack.
- Hybrid AGM and Enterprise Broadcast Suite.
- Product Launch Experience Stack.

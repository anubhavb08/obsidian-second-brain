# Zlicc Campaign Focus

This page converts Zlicc's marketing strategy into active campaign lanes.

## Current Recommended Focus

**Campaign:** Agency Tech Partner Stack  
**Audience:** event agencies and experiential agencies  
**Primary promise:** add high-impact technology layers to event proposals without building the technology internally.  
**Pillars:** [[ZliccPulse]], [[ZliccXR]], [[ZliccStudio]], [[ZliccSync]]

Primary source: [[10 Raw Sources/Zlicc Marketing/Zlicc_CMO_Brain#23 First Campaign Recommendation|First Campaign Recommendation]]

## Campaign Lanes To Maintain

### Lane 1: Agency Tech Partner Stack

- Audience: event and experiential agencies.
- Stack: Pulse, XR, Studio, Sync.
- Needed assets: agency partner deck, WhatsApp pitch card, use-case cards, outreach sequence.
- Status: recommended first focus.

### Lane 2: AI Experience Push

- Audience: brands, product launches, conferences, leadership events, exhibitions, and enterprise communications teams.
- Stack: AI, Studio, Live where relevant.
- Needed assets: ZliccAI experience deck, AI governance checklist, examples of AI in real brand moments.
- Status: next candidate.

### Lane 3: Exhibition And Broadcast Push

- Audience: exhibition agencies, enterprise marketing teams, BFSI, and corporate communications teams.
- Stack: Sync, XR, Pulse, Live, AI, Studio.
- Needed assets: booth intelligence deck, hybrid AGM/webcast one-pager, exhibition lead capture use-case sheet.
- Status: next candidate.

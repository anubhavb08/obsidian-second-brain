# Decisions And Guardrails

This page records cross-vault decisions that should guide future work.

## Vault Decisions

- The vault uses copied source snapshots.
- Raw snapshots live in `10 Raw Sources`.
- Clean working intelligence lives in `20 Wiki`.
- Navigation, logs, and rules live in `00 Control`.
- The first version avoids Obsidian community plugins and heavy automation.

## Source-System Boundaries

- Original Zlicc folders remain source systems.
- Original Personal Growth folders remain source systems.
- This vault organizes intelligence above those systems; it does not rewrite them.
- New wiki pages should link back to snapshots when using source-backed facts.

## Zlicc Guardrails

- Keep Zlicc positioned as an experience infrastructure company.
- Do not make Zlicc sound like a generic event agency, generic AI agency, or generic AV vendor.
- Do not publish, price, promise delivery, use client references, or make technical guarantees without human approval.

Source: [[10 Raw Sources/Zlicc Marketing/Zlicc_CMO_Brain|Zlicc CMO Brain]]

## Personal Growth Guardrails

- Keep [[Growth Automation Studio]] separate from Zlicc unless explicitly comparing.
- Treat `Zandem.ai` as a discussed candidate name only, not a finalized brand.
- Keep commercial pricing and packaging open until deliberately planned.
- Keep outbound actions and campaign changes approval-gated.

Sources:

- [[10 Raw Sources/Personal Growth/docs/overall-plan|Overall Plan]]
- [[10 Raw Sources/Personal Growth/docs/implementation-notes|Implementation Notes]]

## Channel Automation Guardrails

- LinkedIn must remain human-in-loop.
- WhatsApp should use compliant WhatsApp Business API paths only.
- Personal WhatsApp should not be scraped or automated.
- Public publishing and outbound messages require approval gates.
